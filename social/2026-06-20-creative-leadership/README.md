# Creative Leadership Layout Fix

## Cause

- The headline and supporting copy used fixed positions without reserving height for three Korean headline lines.
- Card body copy started too close to the lower edge and allowed three lines, so text crossed card borders.
- The source strip used a larger font than the available horizontal space.

## Fix

- Reserved independent vertical regions for headline, supporting copy, cards, source, and footer.
- Limited each card body to two lines and reduced its type size.
- Shortened and reduced the source-strip type.
