# Medical Animation Source Study

This source pool is used for bbbb.beauty social posts when the content needs to match a medical animation production company, not a generic beauty-market commentary angle.

## Source Types

- Medical animation studio blogs: production use cases, MoA/MoD, device explanation, patient education, HCP training.
- Healthcare content strategy webzines: how animation supports medical product, pharma, hospital, and patient communication.
- Market research summaries: demand signals for pharma marketing, patient education, advanced research, drug MoA, and medical-device communication.
- Studio case studies: how complex scientific stories become accurate, memorable visual narratives.

## Content Rule

Do not use article links as decorative footnotes. Each post should map one source insight to one production claim:

- If the claim is "animation shows what normal video cannot," cite a studio or healthcare animation source.
- If the claim is "patients understand better through visuals," cite patient education or interactive anatomy sources.
- If the claim is "pharma and medical-device demand is growing," cite market research.
- If the claim is "scientific accuracy and memorability both matter," cite studio case studies.
